Why The Speech League

More Than Just Talking

Course curriculum · Grades 2–6

6 Weeks. Built for Real Growth.

A progressive, hands-on curriculum that takes students from nervous beginners to confident, compelling speakers in just 6 weeks.

Week 1

The Confident Speaker Mindset

Understanding nerves, building speaker identity, and learning why public speaking is a superpower worth mastering.

Week 2

Voice, Body & Presence

Vocal variety, posture, eye contact, gestures — and how to use silence like a pro.

Week 3

Storytelling That Sticks

Crafting personal stories with a clear beginning, middle, and ending that captivates any audience.

Week 4

Structuring a Speech

A simple, repeatable framework kids can use for class presentations, debates, and beyond.

Week 5

Connecting With Your Audience

Reading the room, using humour, and making every single listener feel seen and engaged.

Week 6

Live Showcase Performance

A full speech delivered live in front of family and friends — the most exciting day of the course!
